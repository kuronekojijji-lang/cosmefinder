## CANMAKE Eyeshadows by Color Family

### Brown

A versatile color that suits everyone. It naturally defines the eyes and works from natural to polished makeup. If unsure about your first one, brown is recommended.

### Pink

Gives a gentle, cute impression. It adds flush and brightens the complexion. It blends especially well with cool tones—great for those who want soft eyes.

### Orange / Beige

A healthy, approachable color that blends with warm tones. It doesn't lift off the skin and finishes with an effortless impression.

### Purple / Color shades

For when you want a bit of individuality or to add a trendy feel. Used as a deepening shade, it creates effortless, dimensional eyes.

## Recommended by Skin Tone (Personal Color)

| Skin type | Recommended family | Impression |
|---|---|---|
| Warm Spring | Orange, coral brown | Bright, fresh |
| Warm Autumn | Brown, beige | Calm, elegant |
| Cool Summer | Pink, rose | Gentle, translucent |
| Cool Winter | Purple, crisp brown | Glamorous, striking |

If unsure, start with the universally flattering **brown** family.

## CANMAKE Eyeshadow Usage Tips

- **Bright color wide, dark color narrow**: Bright over the socket, deepening shade at the lash line
- **Glitter above the iris**: Placing glow at the center makes eyes look dimensional
- **Layer a little at a time**: Don't apply all at once—adjust to your preferred intensity
