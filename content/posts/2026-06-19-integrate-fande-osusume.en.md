## How to Choose an INTEGRATE Foundation

### Choose by type

- **All-in-one type**: For those who want to save time or finish easily
- **Liquid type**: For those who want moist coverage
- **Powder type**: For those who want a light, smooth finish

### Choose a color that suits your skin tone

For color, **one close to your neck or jawline** blends naturally. Beware overly bright colors that lift—if unsure, choosing a slightly darker shade is the trick.

### Choose by finish

| Finish | Best for |
|---|---|
| Glow / moist | Dry skin, natural fans |
| Semi-matte | Combination skin, polish focus |
| Matte | Oily skin, shine concern |

## INTEGRATE Foundation Usage Tips

- **Build a base with primer**: Improves crease resistance and wear
- **Spread a small amount thinly**: Don't apply thickly—layer only where you want coverage
- **Powder to finish**: Controlling oil-prone areas makes it last
