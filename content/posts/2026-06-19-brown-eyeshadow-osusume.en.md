"Brown eyeshadow" suits everyone and naturally defines the eyes. Having one means a versatile color usable across natural to polished makeup.

In this article, we hand-pick and rank the **best brown eyeshadows**. We also explain how to choose and application tips, so use it as a reference to find your perfect one.

## 3 Points for Choosing a Brown Eyeshadow

### 1. Choose by brown "tone"

There are types of brown. **Light brown** gives a gentle impression; **deep dark brown** gives a defined, mature impression. Choose by the image you want.

### 2. Choose warm/cool undertone to suit your skin

**Warm tones suit yellow-toned browns**, **cool tones suit reddish or gray-leaning browns**. Choosing a tone that suits your skin blends naturally.

### 3. Choose by texture (matte / pearl)

For a natural finish go more matte; for glamour or dimension, choose fine pearl. A palette with multiple textures lets you switch by occasion.

## Brown Eyeshadow Application Tips

- **Bright color wide, dark color narrow**: Bright over the socket, deepening shade at the lash line for a gradient
- **Blend the edges**: Blending between colors with a brush gives a natural finish
- **A little on the lower lash line too**: A deepening shade at the outer corner boosts dimension
