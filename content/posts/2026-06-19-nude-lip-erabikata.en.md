"Nude lip" creates a flattering, effortless impression. Stylish and mature, yet many get stuck choosing it—"it makes me look washed out" or "I don't know my color."

In this article, we clearly explain **how to choose a nude lip**, with points on skin tone and texture. We also introduce picks and usage tips, so if you want to find a flattering nude lip, please use it as a reference.

## What Is a Nude Lip?

A nude lip is a lip in a **beige or skin-like color that blends with your lip color**. It creates an effortless, airy impression without standing out too much. Perfect for subtractive makeup when you want to emphasize the eyes.

But the wrong choice can make the complexion look dull. Choosing a **tone that suits your skin** is the key to wearability.

## 3 Points for Choosing a Nude Lip

### 1. Choose a tone that suits your skin

- **Warm tones**: Yellow/orange-leaning beige, coral nude
- **Cool tones**: Pink/rose-leaning beige, mauve nude

If it doesn't suit your skin, the complexion looks dull—so this is the most important point.

### 2. Choose a color with flush

Rather than a color that fully blends with your skin, choosing a **nude with a hint of flush** keeps the complexion from looking dull and gives a natural airiness.

### 3. Choose by texture (glow / matte)

- **Glow nude**: Hydrated-looking, easy to prevent washed-out flush
- **Matte nude**: A mature, polished impression

Beginners should start with a **glow type** that shows flush easily.

## Tips for Using Nude Lips Well

- **Correct lip color with concealer**: If your lips are dark, lightly covering helps color show
- **Add gloss**: Layering gloss at the center prevents a washed-out flush
- **Make the eyes the star**: With a nude lip, emphasizing the eyes balances it ◎

## Summary

The key to choosing a nude lip is to mind three points: **a tone that suits your skin, a hint of flush, and texture**. Warm tones blend naturally with yellow-leaning beige, cool tones with pink-leaning beige.

If your flush might look washed out, choose a glow type or layer gloss at the center. Try it from an affordable balm lip and enjoy an effortless nude lip that suits you.
