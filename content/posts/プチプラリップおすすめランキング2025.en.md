## How to Choose an Affordable Lipstick

### Choose by finish type

| Type | Trait | Recommended for |
|---|---|---|
| Gloss | Dewy shine | Dryness concern; want plumper-looking lips |
| Matte | Composed impression | Don't want it to break down; want a mature look |
| Tint | Natural flush | Want a bare-faced look; natural makeup |
| Sheer | Translucency, lightness | Office makeup; layering |

### Choose by color family

**For natural makeup:** nude, coral, pink-beige  
**For trendy makeup:** terracotta, burgundy, smoky  
**For office makeup:** rose, mauve, beige-pink
