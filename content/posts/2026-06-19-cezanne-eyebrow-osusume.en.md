## How to Choose a CEZANNE Eyebrow

### Choose color to match your hair

The basics of brow color are **the same as or slightly lighter than your hair**. Light hair pairs with light brown, dark hair with gray or ash tones.

| Hair color | Recommended color |
|---|---|
| Light brown | Light brown family |
| Darker brown | Natural brown family |
| Black hair | Gray / ash family |

### Choose by the impression you want

- **Soft impression**: Soft with a lighter brown
- **Crisp impression**: Define with gray or ash tones

## CEZANNE Eyebrow Usage Tips

- **Light shade at the head, dark shade at the tail**: A natural gradient creates dimension
- **Layer a little at a time**: Don't go dark at once—apply thin then adjust
- **Blend with a spoolie**: Tidying the hair flow while blending looks natural
- **Keep the brow head especially light**: A dark head looks unnatural, so soft is best
