## Skinny Rich Shadow Shades by Type

Skinny Rich Shadow is basically brown-based, with variations by tone difference.

### Light brown

A gentle, natural impression. Flattering and perfect for office makeup or beginners. For those who want soft, gentle eyes.

### Pink / rose brown

A soft impression with flush. It blends with cool tones for feminine eyes.

### Deep brown / smoky

A defined, mature impression. With an effective deepening shade, it's great for those who want definition or for evening makeup.

### Beige / gold brown

An elegant, glamorous impression that blends with warm tones. It doesn't lift off the skin and gives effortless dimension.

## Recommended by Skin Tone (Personal Color)

| Skin type | Recommended family | Impression |
|---|---|---|
| Warm Spring | Light brown, gold | Bright, fresh |
| Warm Autumn | Deep brown, beige | Calm, elegant |
| Cool Summer | Pink, rose brown | Gentle, translucent |
| Cool Winter | Smoky, crisp brown | Glamorous, striking |

If unsure, start with the universally flattering **light brown** family.

## Skinny Rich Shadow Usage Tips

- **In order from top-left to bottom-right**: Just bright over the socket, dark at the lash line completes a gradient
- **Blend the edges**: Blending between colors gives natural dimension
- **A little deepening shade on the lower lash line too**: Adding it at the outer corner boosts depth
