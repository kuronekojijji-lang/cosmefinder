CANMAKE and CEZANNE, the two leading affordable cosmetics brands. Both are available at drugstores with many excellent items, so many wonder, "Which should I choose in the end?"

In this article, we compare **which is better, CANMAKE or CEZANNE**, by brand traits and by item. Use it as a reference for choosing the brand that fits you.

## Basic Differences Between CANMAKE and CEZANNE

First, let's organize each brand's traits.

| Comparison | CANMAKE | CEZANNE |
|---|---|---|
| Price range | Around ¥800 or less | Around ¥700 or less |
| Image | Cute, trendy | Simple, capable |
| Strengths | Blush, eye, cuteness | Primer, practicality, value |
| Packaging | Cute | Simple |
| Color range | Trendy, abundant | Flattery-focused |

Both are excellent and similarly priced, so choosing by **each item's strengths** is recommended.

## Conclusion: Using Both Is the Best Approach

You don't need to pick just one between CANMAKE and CEZANNE. The smartest approach is to use them by strengths: **CEZANNE for base and practical items, CANMAKE for point makeup that adds flush and cuteness**.

Both are affordable, so trying items from both to find what fits you is also a good idea.

## Summary

CANMAKE and CEZANNE are both excellent affordable brands at nearly the same price. The difference is in strengths: **CANMAKE excels at cuteness, trends, and flush; CEZANNE at practicality, value, and base items**.

Use CEZANNE for primer and brows, CANMAKE for blush and lip. Make the most of both brands' strengths and enjoy affordable cosmetics wisely.
