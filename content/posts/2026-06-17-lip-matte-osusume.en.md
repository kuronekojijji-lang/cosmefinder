"Matte lipstick" creates a mature, polished impression. Different from glossy lips, its effortless, on-trend feel is the appeal. But many feel "dryness is a concern" or "color choice is hard."

In this article, we hand-pick and rank the **best matte lipsticks** from affordable to department-store. We also explain how to choose and tips for a beautiful finish, so please use it as a reference.

## 3 Points for Choosing a Matte Lipstick

### 1. Choose "dryness resistance" by hydration

Matte lipstick's drawback is that it dries easily. Choosing one with **moisturizing ingredients** or a smoothly gliding texture keeps a comfortable feel even when matte.

### 2. Check staying power (resistance to fading)

The appeal of matte lipstick is long-lasting color. Choosing a **tint formula or high-adherence type** keeps color even after eating and reduces touch-ups.

### 3. Choose flattering colors

Since matte shows color clearly, choosing a color that suits your skin matters. **Brown and terracotta** finish mature, while **rose and red** finish glamorous.

## Tips for a Beautiful Matte Lip Finish

- **Moisturize with a lip balm first**: Prevents dryness and lets it apply smoothly
- **Prep with exfoliation**: Peeling lips cause uneven color
- **Layer thinly with a brush or finger**: Don't apply all at once—adjust from thin for an even finish
