"Dewy skin" that looks hydrated and lit from within. Glowing, translucent, healthy-looking skin is admired across generations. But many struggle: "I want glow but it looks greasy," or "it breaks down quickly."

So in this article, we hand-pick and rank the **best foundations for dewy skin**, from affordable to department-store. We also explain how to choose a dewy-skin foundation and tips for an elegant glow.

## 3 Points for Choosing a Dewy-Skin Foundation

### 1. Look for a "radiant / glow" finish

To aim for dewy skin, choose a foundation labeled **"radiant," "dewy," or "glow."** It reflects light to create hydrated-looking skin.

### 2. Boost the glow with hydration

When skin is dry, glow is harder to achieve. A foundation **with serum or moisturizing ingredients** keeps a moist glow from within for longer.

### 3. Keep a "bare-skin feel" with thin coverage

Heavy layering kills the glow and looks flat. Choose a **texture that spreads thinly and evenly**, keeping a bare-skin feel—the key to an elegant dewy look.

## Tips for an Elegant Dewy Finish

- **Build a base with hydrating skincare**: Hydrated skin is the foundation of glow
- **Glow at the center, control the outer areas**: Concentrate glow on the high points of the cheeks for dimension
- **Powder on the nose and T-zone**: Matting only the oil-prone areas creates an elegant contrast with the glow

## Summary

The right way to choose a dewy-skin foundation is by three points: **radiant finish, hydration, and thin coverage**.

For both crease resistance and a natural glow, go with "NARS"; for a premium glow, "SUQQU"; to try on a budget, "excel" and "INTEGRATE GRACY." Since glow and grease are a fine line, concentrate the glow on the high points of the cheeks and control the T-zone for an elegant finish. Do get the dewy, glowing skin you admire.
