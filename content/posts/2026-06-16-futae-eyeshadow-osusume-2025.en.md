While double eyelids make eye makeup stand out, you may also wonder: "Because color shows so clearly, the wrong choice can look too bold," or "How do I place color in the crease?"

In this article, we present **recommended eyeshadows for double eyelids** in a ranking alongside 2025's trending colors. We also explain how to choose and apply for double eyelids, so if you want to show off your eyes at their best, please use it as a reference.

## 3 Points for Choosing Eyeshadow for Double Eyelids

### 1. Colors that make the most of payoff

Since color shows clearly on double eyelids, **delicate nuance shades and refined pearl** look great. A palette with easy gradients that makes the most of the payoff is recommended.

### 2. Choose a "deepening shade" balanced with the crease

Because the crease can look wide, applying a **dark deepening shade** tightens the eye. A palette with a brown or smoky deepening shade lets you create defined eyes.

### 3. In 2025, "flush + glow" is in

2025's eye makeup trend is **flush colors** like pink and coral, plus refined **glow and pearl**. Double eyelids show off these trending colors directly, so embrace them actively.

## Eyeshadow Application Tips for Double Eyelids

- **Keep the deepening shade within the crease**: Not over-applying it within the visible range when eyes are open prevents puffiness
- **Spread the base wide**: Spread a bright base shade over the whole socket to build a foundation
- **Glitter above the iris**: Placing glow at the center makes eyes look bigger and more dimensional
