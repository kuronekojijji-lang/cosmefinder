## 3 Points for Choosing an Affordable Foundation

### 1. Choose a texture that suits your skin type

Foundations come in many forms: liquid, powder, cushion, BB cream, and more.

- **Dry skin**: Liquid or cushion foundations with moisturizing ingredients
- **Oily skin**: Powder foundations with oil-absorbing ingredients, or semi-matte liquids
- **Combination skin**: Liquid types that are easy to use on specific areas, or ones you can layer powder over the T-zone

### 2. Decide on the finish you want

Even among affordable options, the finish can vary greatly.

- **Dewy skin**: For those who want light-catching, fresh-looking skin
- **Matte**: For those bothered by oil who want to control shine
- **Natural (semi-matte)**: A balanced type that suits any makeup

### 3. Balance of coverage and ease of use

Since you use it daily, comfort matters. Choose based on your lifestyle—the nozzle, included sponge, and ease of touch-ups.
