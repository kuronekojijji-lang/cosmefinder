As the sunlight softens and hearts lighten, spring arrives. With the new season, you'll want to refresh your makeup to something soft and bright, too.

In this article, we introduce **recommended cosmetics for 2025 spring makeup**, incorporating trends. We also explain this year's spring makeup traits and how to wear them, so if you want a seasonal, on-trend face, please use it as a reference.

## 2025 Spring Makeup Trend: "Flush × Translucency"

The keywords for 2025 spring makeup are **soft flush and clean translucency**. A shift from rich winter makeup, the trend creates a bright, fresh impression with flattering corals and roses.

A dewy-skin base, flush added with blush and lip, and eyes pulled together with gentle brown or pink is the contemporary balance. Not overdone, yet the complexion looks instantly bright—that's the charm of 2025 spring makeup.

## Tips for Wearing Spring Makeup

- **Match colors in the same family**: Pairing blush and lip in corals creates cohesion
- **Make glow the star**: A moist, dewy finish is more spring-like than matte
- **Mind the subtraction**: Don't pile on every part—pull it together naturally with flush and translucency
