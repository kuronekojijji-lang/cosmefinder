## How to Choose Marshmallow Finish Powder Shades

### Choose by skin tone (brightness)

- **Brighter skin**: Light ochre or pink blends well
- **Standard skin**: Natural ochre or beige is versatile
- **Healthy skin**: A slightly deeper beige for cohesion

Choosing one close to your foundation color avoids color lift when layered, for a natural finish.

### Choose by finish preference

- **Want translucency**: Pink or lavender for flush and dullness correction
- **Want to naturally even out skin tone**: Ochre or beige
- **Want to mattify**: A beige close to your skin tone, evenly

### Matte type vs. glitter type

Marshmallow Finish comes in a **matte type** plus a type that adds faint glow. Choose matte to control shine, or a glow type if you want natural sheen.

## Marshmallow Finish Powder Usage Tips

- **After primer/foundation**: Applying as a finish controls oil and boosts wear
- **Lightly with the included puff**: A small amount from the face center outward
- **Layer on the T-zone**: Layer on oil-prone areas for smoothness
- **For breakdown fixes**: Blot oil with paper, then layer
