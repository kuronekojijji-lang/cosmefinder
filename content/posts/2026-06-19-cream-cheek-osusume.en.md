"Cream blush" adheres to skin and creates a natural flush that seems to come from within. With a glowy finish different from powder, it's essential for contemporary skin.

In this article, we hand-pick and rank the **best cream blushes**. We also explain how to choose and application tips, so if you want a natural flush, please use it as a reference.

## 3 Points for Choosing a Cream Blush

### 1. Choose by texture

The **softer, more spreadable** a cream blush, the easier it blends and the harder it is for beginners to mess up. Choose one easy to pat in with your fingers.

### 2. Choose colors that suit your skin

Warm tones suit **coral and orange**, cool tones **pink and rose**, making the complexion brighter.

### 3. Choose by finish (glow / natural)

Cream blush's charm is a glowy flush. For a contemporary glowy finish, a hydrating type is recommended.

## Cream Blush Application Tips

- **Take a little on your fingertip**: Too much gets heavy, so add gradually
- **Pat at the high point of the cheek**: Place it where the cheek rises when you smile
- **Blend toward the outside**: Gently blend so the edges disappear
- **After foundation, before powder**: It adheres better for a natural finish
