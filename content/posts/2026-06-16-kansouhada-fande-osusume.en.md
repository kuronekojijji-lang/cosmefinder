"By evening, my fine lines look dry and flaky." "My foundation cakes up and sits on top of my skin." If you have dry skin, finding a foundation with the right glow and adherence can feel like an endless struggle.

In this article, we introduce the **best foundations for dry skin** in a ranking, hand-picked from drugstore buys to department-store gems. We also explain how to choose, so you can find the one that suits your skin.

## 3 Points for Choosing a Foundation for Dry Skin

### 1. Choose one with moisturizing ingredients

If you have dry skin, look first for a **foundation that contains moisturizing ingredients** like hyaluronic acid, collagen, or serum components. Skincare-type foundations support your skin's moisture even while you wear makeup.

### 2. Go for a dewy, moist finish

Matte types control oil but tend to highlight dryness. For dry skin, we recommend a **dewy, moist liquid or cushion foundation** that looks lit from within.

### 3. A thin, buildable texture

Heavy layering causes dryness-related cracking. Choose a **texture that spreads thinly and adheres closely**, and layer only where needed to keep your makeup looking fresh longer.

## Tips to Keep Foundation From Cracking on Dry Skin

- **Build a moisture base with skincare**: Prep with toner and emulsion before foundation
- **Layer thinly**: Don't apply thickly at once—add a little only where needed
- **Use a hydrating primer**: Add a moisture barrier with a primer before foundation
