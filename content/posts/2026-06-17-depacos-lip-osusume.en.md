There are moments when you want a step-up "department-store lipstick" for a treat to yourself or a special day's makeup. Beautiful color payoff, premium glow, a texture that lifts your mood just by owning it—a satisfaction a notch above affordable picks is the charm of department-store lipsticks.

In this article, we hand-pick and rank the **best department-store lipsticks**. We also explain how to choose, so if you're looking for a premium one, please use it as a reference.

## 3 Points for Choosing a Department-Store Lipstick

### 1. Choose by finish (glow / matte)

Department-store lipsticks come in many finishes. **Moist glow types** look glamorous and elegant, while **matte types** give a mature, polished impression. Choose by the image you want.

### 2. Care for lips with moisture & beauty ingredients

Higher-priced department-store options often lavishly contain **serum and moisturizing ingredients**. Caring for your lips even while wearing it is a charm unique to department-store cosmetics.

### 3. Color range and how it flatters

Department-store brands offer **exquisite color ranges** that suit your skin. Choosing a color that matches your tone (warm/cool) makes the complexion instantly brighter.

## Tips to Enjoy Department-Store Lipstick Longer

- **Prime with a lip balm**: Moisturizing before applying improves both payoff and wear
- **Tissue-off for adherence**: Apply once, lightly blot, and reapply to improve color hold
- **Outline with a brush**: A department-store's beautiful payoff stands out even more with brush work
