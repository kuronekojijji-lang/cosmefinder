## CANMAKE Cream Cheek Shades by Color Family

CANMAKE Cream Cheek can be broadly divided into 4 color families. Let's look at each one's traits and impression.

### Pink

The classic flush color. Creates a translucent, cute impression. It blends well with **cool-tone** skin for a soft, gentle vibe. Perfect for natural makeup, too.

### Coral

Between pink and orange, a versatile color that suits almost anyone. It gives a healthy, bright impression and is easy to use **for both warm and cool tones**. Handy to have one.

### Orange

A color that creates a lively, fresh impression. It blends with **warm-tone** skin for a healthy, upbeat vibe. Recommended for casual or summer makeup.

### Red / Rose

Creates a mature, glamorous impression. Perfect when you want a strong flush or a polished look. Since a little is highly pigmented, the trick is to pat it on thinly.

## How to Choose by Skin Tone (Personal Color)

| Skin type | Recommended family | Impression |
|---|---|---|
| Warm Spring | Coral, orange | Bright, fresh |
| Warm Autumn | Orange, terracotta | Healthy, calm |
| Cool Summer | Pink, rose | Translucent, gentle |
| Cool Winter | Blue-pink, red | Glamorous, crisp |

If unsure, start with the universally flattering **coral** family.

## Tips for Using CANMAKE Cream Cheek Well

- **A little on the fingertip**: Too much gets heavy, so add gradually
- **Pat at the high point of the cheek**: Place it where the cheek rises when you smile
- **Blend toward the outside**: Gently blend so the edges disappear
- **Layer powder**: Adding powder blush or face powder on top boosts wear
