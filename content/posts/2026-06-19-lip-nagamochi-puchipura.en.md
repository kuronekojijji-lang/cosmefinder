"The color was gone after lunch," "I have no time for touch-ups"—many struggle with lipstick fading when they want to keep clean lips even at work.

So in this article, we hand-pick and rank the **best long-lasting affordable lipsticks**. We also explain how to choose a fade-resistant lipstick and tips to improve hold, so if you want to reduce touch-ups, please use it as a reference.

## 3 Points for Choosing a Long-Lasting Lipstick

### 1. Choose a tint formula

A **tint formula** that colors as if staining the lips keeps color even after eating. If you prioritize longevity, choosing a tint type first is recommended.

### 2. Check high-adherence matte types

A **matte to semi-matte type** that clings closely to the lips tends to hold color better than glossy types. For fade resistance, choose one with high adherence.

### 3. Don't forget hydration

Some long-lasting types dry easily, so one with **moisturizing ingredients** or a hydrating texture is comfortable. The ideal is choosing by the balance of fade resistance and moisture.

## Tips to Make Lipstick Last

- **Moisturize & exfoliate before applying**: Peeling causes uneven color and fading
- **Tissue-off and reapply**: Apply once, blot, and reapply to adhere and boost hold
- **A light dusting of face powder**: Lightly powdering the lips before applying helps it set
- **Use a lip liner for the outline**: Prevents color fading from the edges
