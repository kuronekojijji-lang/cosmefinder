## List of Cosmetics Needed for Natural Makeup

To assemble natural makeup, these 6 are the basics.

1. **Sunscreen / primer** … Protects skin and forms the foundation base
2. **Foundation / powder** … Naturally evens out the skin
3. **Eyebrow** … Softly shapes the brows
4. **Eyeshadow** … Adds natural shadow to the eyes
5. **Blush** … Adds flush
6. **Lip** … Finishes with a natural flush

Gathering them all affordably, a full natural makeup set comes together for **around ¥6,000 total**.

## Tips to Make Natural Makeup Look Great

- **Prioritize flattering colors**: Pull it together with natural colors that suit your skin, not bold ones
- **Mind the subtraction**: Don't darken everything—keep it natural and thin
- **Value the flush**: Adding a natural flush with blush and lip looks healthy
