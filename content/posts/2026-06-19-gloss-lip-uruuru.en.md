## 3 Points for Choosing a Dewy Gloss Lip

### 1. Choose by gloss texture

There are types of gloss. **Dewy clear gloss** gives an airy, contemporary impression; **strong gloss** gives a plump, voluminous impression. Choose by the image you want.

### 2. Check non-stickiness

You want dewiness but not stickiness. Choosing a **smooth, light feel** reduces the stress of hair sticking to your lips.

### 3. Care for lips with moisture & beauty ingredients

Since gloss stays on the lips a long time, one with **moisturizing ingredients** lets you care while wearing. A point especially worth checking if dryness bothers you.

## Tips for Using Dewy Gloss Lips Well

- **Moisturize before applying**: Priming with a lip balm helps it glide
- **Dab at the center**: Concentrating glow at the lip center boosts dimension
- **Don't over-layer**: Too much creases easily, so use the right amount
- **Layer over lipstick too**: Layering over your favorite color adds dewy gloss
