"I want to start makeup, but what cosmetics should I buy first?" When you decide to make your makeup debut, there are so many types that it's easy to get lost over what to gather.

In this article, we clearly explain **what cosmetics beginners should buy first**, with priorities and budget guidance. We've gathered the first essentials and foolproof tips for choosing, so use it as a reference for your makeup debut.

## 5 Essentials Beginners Should Gather First

The first 5 a makeup beginner should buy are below. Gathering them in this order completes a full natural makeup.

1. **Sunscreen / primer** … Protects skin and forms a base for better foundation wear
2. **Foundation / face powder** … Evens out and refines the skin
3. **Eyebrow** … Shapes the brows that set the face's impression
4. **Blush** … Makes the complexion bright and healthy
5. **Lip** … Finishes the whole face with a flourish

Gathering them all affordably, you can make your makeup debut for **around ¥5,000 total**.

## Foolproof Tips for Choosing Cosmetics

- **Start with affordable**: Rather than expensive right away, try cheap first to check if it suits you
- **Prioritize flattering colors**: Start with natural colors that suit your skin, not bold ones
- **Gather gradually**: Don't buy everything at once—start with the higher priorities
