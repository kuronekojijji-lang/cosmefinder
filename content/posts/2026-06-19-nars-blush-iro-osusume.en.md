## NARS Blush Shades by Color Family

### Pink

An elegant, glamorous impression. It adds translucency and instantly brightens the complexion. Especially flattering for cool tones.

### Coral

Between pink and orange, a versatile color that suits almost anyone. It gives a healthy, bright impression and is easy to use for both warm and cool tones.

### Peach

A soft, feminine impression. Flattering and perfect when you want a soft flush. Works for both natural and glamorous looks.

### Rose

A mature, refined impression. Recommended for polished makeup or scenes where you want to look glamorous.

## Recommended by Skin Tone (Personal Color)

| Skin type | Recommended family | Impression |
|---|---|---|
| Warm Spring | Coral, peach | Bright, fresh |
| Warm Autumn | Coral, terracotta | Healthy, calm |
| Cool Summer | Pink, rose | Translucent, elegant |
| Cool Winter | Rose, crisp pink | Glamorous, striking |

If unsure, start with the universally flattering **coral** family.

## Tips for Using NARS Blush Well

- **Take a little on the brush**: Highly pigmented, so don't take too much—a little at a time
- **Tap off once on a tissue**: Prevents going too heavy
- **From the high point of the cheek outward**: Softly at the high point of a smiling cheek
- **Match blush and lip**: Coordinating in the same family creates cohesion
