## How to Choose a CANMAKE Lip

### Choose by finish

- **Moist glow finish**: A hydrating balm type for a natural flush
- **Bold color**: A strongly pigmented type for glamour

### Choose color by skin tone

| Skin type | Recommended color | Impression |
|---|---|---|
| Warm Spring | Coral, peach | Bright, fresh |
| Warm Autumn | Terracotta, brown | Calm, mature |
| Cool Summer | Rose, pink | Gentle, translucent |
| Cool Winter | Red, blue-pink | Glamorous, crisp |

If unsure, start with the universally flattering **coral** family.

## Tips for Using CANMAKE Lips Well

- **Moisturize with a lip balm first**: Improves both payoff and wear
- **Layer thinly to adjust**: Don't apply all at once—layer to your preferred intensity
- **Match color with your blush**: Same-family lip and blush create cohesion
