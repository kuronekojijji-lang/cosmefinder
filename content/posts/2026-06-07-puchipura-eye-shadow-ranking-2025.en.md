## 3 Points for Choosing an Affordable Eyeshadow

### 1. Choose by the number and combination of shades

Affordable eyeshadows come in single, two-color, four-color, and multi-shade palettes. For beginners, a **4-shade set** is recommended. It usually includes a base, shading, deepening, and highlight color, so a single palette completes a gradient.

### 2. Choose by texture (matte, shimmer, glitter)

- **Matte**: Best for crisp definition and natural makeup
- **Shimmer (pearl)**: Adds natural sheen and depth—great for daily use
- **Glitter**: A glamorous finish that shines for nights out and parties

### 3. Choose flattering shades

Brown, pink-beige, and terracotta tones are said to suit Japanese skin well. More and more brands now clearly label shades for "cool tones" or "warm tones," so choosing by your personal color reduces mistakes.
